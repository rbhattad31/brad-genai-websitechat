About

Classic BnB, a subsidiary of Classic Properties, a renowned real estate brokerage and property management company in  UAE, is dedicated to redefining the vacation rental experience. Leveraging our expertise in the industry, we have established Classic BnB to provide exceptional “Bed and Breakfast” experiences in UAE. With a passion for outstanding hospitality, we strive to offer our guests unforgettable stays in handpicked properties, including vacation homes, apartments, and luxury villas.
Committed to personalized service and attention to detail, we aim to exceed expectations for family holidays and guests seeking vacation homes in UAE. We meticulously curate a diverse collection of holiday homes, ensuring each property meets our high standards of quality, comfort, and style.
As a part of Classic Properties, we benefit from a deep understanding of the real estate market in UAE and the needs of property owners. Our mission is to create mutually beneficial partnerships, allowing property owners to maximize their rental potential while providing guests with remarkable experiences, making us the preferred choice for “Bed and Breakfast” in UAE.

Our Vision

To become the preferred alternative to traditional vacation rental platforms in UAE, providing a seamless and personalized experience for guests and property owners alike, whether they are looking for apartments, vacation homes, or family holidays.




Our Mission

We aim to offer a curated collection of holiday homes in UAE, delivering exceptional service, and fostering mutually beneficial partnerships with property owners. Our commitment is to ensure every guest enjoys a memorable stay while property owners maximize their rental potential through Classic BnB.
